The Lord of Opium



Nancy Farmer

Synopsis

Matt is the clone of El Patron, a drug lord who ruled over Opium, a nation on the border between what is currently the United States of America and Mexico. After El Patron's death, Matt is left in charge but alone to fend for himself. Opium relies on microchipped slaves called eejits for labor needs. Matt sees how inhumane the whole process was, and as a clone whose organs were meant to be harvested, he was unable to let this continue. Matt sets out on a journey to free the eejits while trying to outsmart his enemies, who are after El Patron's wealth and power.


Meet The Characters

Mateo "Matt" Alcran

Matt was originally born as a clone, a source of organ transplants for El Patron, who was over a hundred years old. Normally, clones were supposed to be given a drug to cripple their minds, but it wasn't given to Matt. In the previous book, The House of the Scorpion, Matt was given poison that stayed in his organs, making him unsuitable as a source of organs. After the funeral, Matt was the only Alcran alive, and so he became the new drug lord.

El Patron

El Patron founded his drug empire during the last 1900s or early 2000s. He owned vast poppy fields in a country called Opium, situated where the current-day border between Mexico and the US lies. El Patron relied on organs from clones to continue to survive, and created microchips to control captured slaves, known as eejits. El Patron also created the Scorpion Star, a space station, and a sun observatory dedicated to protecting the Star. He ruled without mercy and once shot down a passenger plane that flew into Opium as a way to assert his power.

The Eejits

Eejits are slaves, usually people captured while trying to cross the border between the US/Mexico and Opium. These slaves have microchips planted in their brains. Some chips allowed the slaves to repeat tasks for hours without rest, at the cost of removing almost all of their intelligence and identity. Other chips, such as those planted in Cienfugos, allow thinking and freedom but prevent the person from leaving Opium or harming El Patron. The eejits are responsible for tending to the poppy fields and serving the Alcran family, but are not seen as humans.

Cienfugos

Cienfugos is the head of the Farm Patrol, the organization inside Opium responsible for capturing those attempting to cross and microchipping them. After the El Patron's death, he begins to serve Matt. Cienfugos helps Matt establish authority and protects him from his enemies. Cienfugos supports Matt's goal of ending drug trade.
